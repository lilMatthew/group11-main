Here are 9 ways to enjoy your free time

  1. Reading fiction: It’s better to binge a novel than to binge an entire TV-Show. why? It magnifies your imagination.
  2. Volunteer: If you have free time, why not volunteer for a cause or NGO? You will meet new people and enjoy your time.
  3. Friends: Introvert or extrovert we are social creatures at our core. We need people around us. So why not hang out with your friends?
  4. Talking to strangers: I am not talking about being a creep but go to a new restaurant and try striking a conversation. It will be exciting!
  5. Sports: Free time? Play a sport you like. It’s also good for your body.
  6. Cook: Try making a new dish. It’s as easy as playing a youtube video, right?
  7. Chess: It a good exercise for your brain and will make you smart.
  8. Music: It’s always a great idea to listen to the music you love.
  9. Story writing: Try writing a story about what happened recently. It doesn’t even have to be good.
